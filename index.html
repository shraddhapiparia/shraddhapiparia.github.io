Shraddha Piparia, Ph.D.

Researchineer & Computational Biologist

I’m a Post-Doctoral Scientist in the Department of Pediatrics, UC San Diego, having earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of North Texas in 2021. My research bridges statistical genetics, natural-language processing and machine learning to advance precision medicine for childhood asthma.:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

About Me

I specialise in applying AI/ML and NLP techniques to respiratory-disease datasets with a current focus on genotype-phenotype associations for asthma in children. My long-term goal is to integrate multi-omics with clinical data to deliver ancestry-aware pharmacogenomic tools.:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
