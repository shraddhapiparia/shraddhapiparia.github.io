TITLE OF RESEARCH STUDY: An Exploratory Analysis of Mobile Application Context Events Occurring during Regular Day-to-Day Use.

This study involves the collection of data regarding the occurrence of context events (e.g. changes in network availability, battery power levels, location, plugging and unplugging input and output devices, etc.) from your mobile phone. The collected data will be used to determine real-world sequences of context events that could potentially affect the functionality and testing of mobile applications.
